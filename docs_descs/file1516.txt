What is the difference between a while statement and a do while statement?
 Answer : 
 A while statement checks at the beginning of a loop to see whether the next loop  iteration should occur. 
 A do while statement checks at the end of a loop to see whether the next iteration of a loop should occur. 
 The do whilestatement will always execute the body of a loop at least once.
