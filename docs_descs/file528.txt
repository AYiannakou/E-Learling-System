Course Title: Object-Oriented Programming

Course Code: CBOP3203

Course Description:

CBOP3203 Object-Oriented Programming,This course is a three-credit, middle-level course on 

computer programming within the Bachelor of Information Technology and other IT-related programs 

offered by the faculty. This is a self-contained course. Thus, you are not required to have basic 

knowledge in programming.
