MANILA, Philippines--Lim Teow Siang, chief operation adviser of Sun Microsystems Philippines, Inc., talks about the current state of Java and the future direction for the platform.

Lim was responding to a question from INQUIRER.net gaming and multimedia editor Joey Alarilla during a dinner with Filipino tech journalists on March 12, 2007 to mark the 25th anniversary of Sun.
